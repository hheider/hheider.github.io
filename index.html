Welcome to Ice Age Nordic Ski Club!

Ice Age Nordic Cross Country Ski Club has opportunities for everyone to get involved in cross-country skiing. From competitive racing opportunities on our High School Ski Team, to coaching and learn-to-ski programs with our Middle School Team, Youth Ski Program, and Adult Training Group, and a fun, encouraging environment all around -- you're sure to find something for everyone to enjoy.

Based in Lodi, WI, we serve the surrounding area of south-central Wisconsin north of Madison, including Dane, Waunakee, and Deforest.

We are currently gearing up for the 2022-2023 season and praying for some snow!

Skiers, see our Team App webpage for the most recent updates and events.
