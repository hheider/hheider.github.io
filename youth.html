Youth Ski Program

Our Youth Ski Program is open to kids in 1st-5th grade. It provides a fun introduction to classic-style cross country skiing. We put a strong emphasis on safety and skills while still having fun, playing games, and goofing around on skis. Oh -- and don't forget the hot chocolate afterwards!

Practices are held on Mondays after school at the Lodi golf course trails from December through February.

The Youth Ski Program is led by Paula Tonn. Members of our High School Team assist with coaching each year, and adult volunteers are always welcome to help.

Registration includes equipment loaned for the entire winter in addition to the weekly sessions.
